At MindFlow, we design our architecture offline-first. Your relaxation, breathing habits, and score progress are primary to you, and we prioritize giving you absolute transparency and ownership of your records.

1. Data Custody Summary

  • Local Storage: All profile settings, streaks, levels, and completed session notes are stored directly inside your browser cache. No account is required to play.
  • Cloud Syncing: If you sign in, progress parameters (experience level, calm metrics, session durations, and environment logs) are transmitted securely using JSON web tokens (JWT) to your account on our cloud database so you can sync progress across devices.
Wellness Disclaimer: MindFlow is designed to guide relaxing breathing patterns and attention training for study breaks or stress relief. It is a wellness experience, not a clinical treatment, and does not record HIPAA-regulated medical diagnoses.

2. Your Data Sovereignty Rights

Under regulations like GDPR, you hold rights over your personal data. We expose these controls directly below so you can audit or erase your data at any time.
